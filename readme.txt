Quickly reference the pages that you've edited recntly, just under the "Pages" -> "All Pages" menu item.

== Description ==

Ever want to quickly edit one of the last few pages you've been working on?  Hate having to first click on "Page" and then wade through your list of pages to find what you want to edit?

Then look no further.  "Recent Edits" puts a list of the last 5 pages you've worked on, right under the "All Pages" menu item in the "Pages" submenu.


== Installation ==

1. Upload `recent-edits` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, or upload the provided zip through your WordPress Dashboard "Plugin" panel.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
